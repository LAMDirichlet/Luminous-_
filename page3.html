Why you should avoid getting into these kind of troubles!

Being one of the victims of these problems can destroy your life. This might ruin your oppurtunity of having a decent job
that pays well. It might lead someone's mind to depression and eventually lead to suicide. That's why it's very importany for us to
protect ourselves from the harms the internet might bring.

Here's some tips how to avoid being the next victims:

Identity Theft

  • Use Strong, Unique Passwords
  • Two-Factor Authetication (2FA)
  • Monitor Accounts
  • Avoid Sharing Personal Information

Digital Footprint

  • Be Mindful of What you Post
  • Review Privacy Settings
  • Delete Unnecessary Accounts
  • Google Yourself

Online Scams

  • Be Cautious with Emails and Links
  • Verify Sources
  • Use Trusted Payment Methods
  • Keep Software Updated

Cyberbullying

  • Set Boundaries
  • Report and Block
  • Seek Support
  • Document Abuse

Misinformation

  • Fact-check Before Sharing
  • Question Sources
  • Educate Yourself on Media Literacy
  • Limit Echo Chambers
