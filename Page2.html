Identity Theft
Lt. Jessa Mendoza had experienced identity theft which led to many case reports about her. She was shocked upon hearing the news that
her name and tax identification number were listed in the incorporation papers of 193 corporations, 102 of which sounded foreign
to her. Eventually, she recalled during a Senate probe on illegal offshore gaming operators (POGOs) how she was slapped with cases
involving companies whose names were totally unknown to her.
